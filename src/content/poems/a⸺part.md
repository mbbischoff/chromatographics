thin string between us
stretching miles
connecting our chests

<p style="text-align: right;">
tension we can sense
tugging twine
winding over land
</p>

we set it aside
knotted tight
when we’re together

<p style="text-align: right;">
the rope uniting us
untying now
because we’re home
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
we don’t need help
to touch
when we’re here
</p>
